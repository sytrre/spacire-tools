About This Tool ▼

The Science of Bedtime Routines

A consistent bedtime routine is one of the most effective, evidence-based strategies for improving sleep quality. Research from the Sleep Foundation and Harvard Medical School shows that wind-down routines help signal to your body that sleep is approaching, triggering physiological changes that make falling asleep easier.

Why Routines Work

  • Circadian cueing: Consistent activities at consistent times reinforce your body's natural sleep-wake cycle
  • Cortisol reduction: Relaxing activities lower stress hormones that interfere with sleep
  • Mental transition: Routines create a psychological boundary between day and night
  • Sleep pressure: Screen-free time allows natural melatonin production
